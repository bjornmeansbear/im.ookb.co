MICA SOCIAL DESIGN

Through collaborative, hands-on opportunities, the Master of Arts in Social Design (MASD) prepares students to play a vital role in positively impacting our world by exploring the designer′s role and responsibility in society, producing new ways of thinking, facilitating new institutional collaborations, utilizing design to bring about social change, and developing contexts in which ideas can be sustained.

GBTC

Accelerators and angel investors; cool events attracting diverse and creative people with world-changing ideas; new entrepreneurs making their presence known quickly and efficiently; young companies with amazing products; established firms and public organizations increasing their commitments to foment innovation and growth; oftern praised for leading collaboartion in tech sphere.

Johns Hopkins | SIL

The Social Innovation Lab leverages the unique strengths, skills, and resources of Baltimore, Johns Hopkins, and MICA to transform nascent concepts into tangible social impact. The Lab is led by a multidisciplinary team dedicated to sharing their experiences and improving the world around them. Events inlcude meetups, workshops, speakers, and an annual Pitch Day.

IndieReader

a venue for discriminating book-lovers to find and purchase books published by the people who wrote them. Think indie movies–produced with a singular vision–instead of the mass-marketed stuff you’d find at the multiplex.

Red Emma's

a worker-owned and collectively-managed bookstore and coffeehouse located in Baltimore's Mount Vernon neighborhood. books and periodicals on a wide range of topics, with a focus on radical politics and culture. We also offer free internet access, both through our wireless network and our public internet terminals.

Fusion Partnership

Serve as an incubator and fiscal sponsor for grassroots community based programs and projects working for social justice; Facilitate programs that build community and collaboration for social change.
